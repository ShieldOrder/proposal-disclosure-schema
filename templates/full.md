# Proposal Disclosure Schema (Full)

This template is opt-in. It is intended to improve clarity and verification, not to enforce compliance.

## A. Summary

A1. One-line summary  
A2. Problem statement  
A3. Target users and success criteria

## B. Scope

B1. In scope  
B2. Out of scope  
B3. Non-goals

## C. Deliverables and Milestones

C1. Deliverables list  
C2. Milestones with acceptance criteria  
C3. Milestone dependencies  
C4. Demo or artifact requirements

## D. Budget

D1. Total request  
D2. Budget breakdown  
D3. Payment structure (if milestone-based, define triggers)  
D4. Cost risks and contingencies

## E. Dependencies and Assumptions

E1. External dependencies  
E2. Internal assumptions  
E3. Key constraints

## F. Risks

F1. Technical risks  
F2. Execution risks  
F3. Operational risks  
F4. Security and privacy considerations (if applicable)  
F5. Mitigations and residual risk

## G. Verification

G1. What is verifiable vs discretionary  
G2. Verification artifacts (repos, builds, docs, deployments)  
G3. Reproducibility expectations (if applicable)  
G4. Reporting cadence and format (if applicable)

## H. Operations and Maintenance

H1. Maintenance plan  
H2. Ownership and handoff  
H3. Upgrade path and deprecation plan

## I. Prior Work and Credibility

I1. Prior shipped work  
I2. References to related efforts  
I3. Team background (optional)

## J. Conflicts and Interfaces

J1. Conflicts of interest (if any)  
J2. Custody interfaces (if any)  
J3. Governance or committee touchpoints (if any)

## K. Appendices (Optional)

K1. Threat model  
K2. Test plan  
K3. Benchmarks
EOF
