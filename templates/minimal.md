# Proposal Disclosure Schema (Minimal)

This template is opt-in. It is intended to improve clarity and verification, not to enforce compliance.
This disclosure template is voluntary and descriptive.
Completing this template does not imply approval, compliance, or endorsement.
The template is intended to improve clarity and reduce ambiguity for reviewers and observers.
Omission or variation does not constitute deficiency.

## 1. One-line Summary
What are you building or delivering.

## 2. Problem and Target User
What problem is solved and for whom.

## 3. Deliverables
List the concrete deliverables.

## 4. Timeline
Start date, milestone dates, end date.

## 5. Budget Request
Amount requested and what it covers.

## 6. Dependencies
External dependencies or upstream requirements.

## 7. Key Risks
Top risks that could prevent delivery.

## 8. Verification
How a third party can verify delivery using observable artifacts.

## 9. Maintenance
What happens after completion. Who maintains it and for how long.

## 10. Previous Work
Links to relevant prior work or shipped artifacts.
