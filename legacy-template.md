## Reference Notice

This disclosure template is optional and descriptive.

Completing this template does not imply approval, compliance, or endorsement.
Omission, partial completion, or variation does not constitute deficiency.

This template exists to surface assumptions, constraints, risks, and verification surfaces for clearer evaluation and observation.

Copy this template into your proposal document and complete it there.
Do not complete it on the webpage.
